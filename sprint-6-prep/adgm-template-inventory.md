## Patterns Observed

What makes existing ADGM templates work — for TemplateDiscoveryAgent prompt design:

- **Section density:** templates average 5 sections; the registry is mature ADGM-focused content.
- **SectionSpec shape:** each section carries `section_id` (snake_case), `title`, `required_provisions`/coverage refs, `drafting_instructions`, `render_mode`.
- **AppendixSpec integration:** `DocumentTemplate.appendices` is wired (I2) — static-form appendices (conflicts register, contact details) render via TABLE/TWO_COLUMN modes.
- **Anti-fabrication:** `entity_fact_questions` on templates feed the preflight questionnaire so Claude injects confirmed facts rather than guessing.
